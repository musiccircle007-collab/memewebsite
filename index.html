Drift - Stoic Journal

Live on Play Store

Drift is a minimalist journaling application designed around the principles of Stoicism. It encourages users to reflect and find clarity. To enhance the experience, it incorporates subtle Machine Learning and NLP features to understand journal entries and intelligently set context-aware reminders, helping users stay consistent with their self-reflection goals.

Teehee

Closed Testing

Teehee is a next-generation reminders app focused purely on the power of Natural Language Processing. The core feature is its ability to understand complex, conversational sentences for setting reminders. For example, you can type "remind me to call mom every other Tuesday at 8pm" and Teehee will parse and set it up correctly, eliminating the need for cumbersome menus and date pickers.
